<h1>Scones</h1>
<p style="color: #888; margin-top: -0.5em;">12 min bake, 220°C</p>

## Ingredients

<pre style="padding: 1em; border-radius: 4px; display: inline-block; margin: 0; color: #000;"><span style="color: #888;">Makes 8-10</span>

plain flour      350g
baking powder    10g
unsalted butter  85g
caster sugar     50g
egg              55g
whole milk       120g
salt             3g

<span style="color: #888;">Egg wash</span>
egg              1
</pre>

<span style="display:block; color:#888; font-size:0.8em; margin-top:0.8em; border-left: 2px solid #E5DECF; padding-left: 0.6em;">Butter must be cold. Handle the dough as little as possible or they'll be tough.<br>Press the cutter straight down without twisting or they won't rise evenly.<br>Best eaten the same day.</span>

## Recipe

**1.** Heat the oven to 220°C and line a baking tray.

**2.** Mix the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Rub in the cold butter until it looks like breadcrumbs.

**3.** Beat the egg and milk together. Add to the mix and stir until it just comes together. Stop as soon as it does.

**4.** Tip onto a floured surface and pat to about 2.5cm thick. Don't roll it.

**5.** Cut out rounds with a cutter, pressing straight down. Re-pat any offcuts and cut again.

**6.** Place on the tray and brush the tops with beaten egg.

**7.** Bake for 12-15 minutes until well risen and golden.
