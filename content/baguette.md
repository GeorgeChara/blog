<h1>Baguette</h1>
<p style="color: #888; margin-top: -0.5em;">recipe v1.3 · 5 min prep · 25 min bake</p>

## Ingredients

<span style="color: #888; font-size: 0.9em;">Makes 4 baguettes</span>

```
900g     flour
580ml    water
50ml     milk
2 tbsp   extra virgin olive oil
3 tsp    crushed Maldon sea salt
1/2 tsp  dry yeast
```

## Recipe

Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Mix until just combined. The dough should be shaggy, not smooth.

<details>
<summary>show photo</summary>

![Mix all ingredients](/images/baguette/1-mix-all.jpg)

</details>

Cover and leave to rise for 8 to 10 hours, or overnight. The dough should double or triple in size.

<details>
<summary>show photos</summary>

<div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: 1fr 1fr; gap: 8px; margin: 1em 0;">
  <img src="/images/baguette/2-let-rise-with-lid.jpg" alt="Before rising" style="width: 100%; height: 250px; object-fit: cover; border-radius: 4px;">
  <img src="/images/baguette/3-morning-result-of-rise.jpg" alt="After rising" style="width: 100%; height: 250px; object-fit: cover; border-radius: 4px;">
</div>

</details>

Preheat the oven to 230°C.

Dust flour generously over the worktop. Carefully turn the dough out, keeping as many bubbles intact as possible.

<details>
<summary>show photos</summary>

![Flour the work surface](/images/baguette/4-flour-work-surface.jpg)

![Dough on work surface](/images/baguette/6-dough-on-work-surface.jpg)

</details>

Dust more flour over the top of the dough.

<details>
<summary>show photo</summary>

![Flour on top of dough](/images/baguette/7-flour-on-dough.jpg)

</details>

Divide into 4 equal pieces. Gently shape each piece into a baguette. Avoid pressing out the air.

<details>
<summary>show photo</summary>

![Shape into baguettes](/images/baguette/8-make-into-baguette-shapes.jpg)

</details>

Turn them seam-side down onto a baking tray lined with baking paper. Stretch gently to final length.

<details>
<summary>show photo</summary>

![Ready for the oven](/images/baguette/9-put-onto-rack.jpg)

</details>

Bake for 25 to 30 minutes until golden and hollow-sounding when tapped on the base.
