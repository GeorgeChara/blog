<h1>Baguette</h1>
<p style="color: #888; margin-top: -0.5em;">recipe v1.3 · 5 min prep · 25 min bake</p>

## Ingredients

<pre style="padding: 1em; border-radius: 4px;"><span style="color: #888;">Makes 4 baguettes</span>

900g     flour
580ml    water
50ml     milk
2 tbsp   extra virgin olive oil
3 tsp    crushed Maldon sea salt
1/2 tsp  dry yeast
</pre>

## Recipe

**1.** Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Mix until all ingredients are combined.

<details>
<summary>show photo</summary>

![Mix all ingredients](/images/baguette/1-mix-all.jpg)

</details>

**2.** Cover and leave to rise for 8 to 10 hours, or overnight.

<details>
<summary>show photos</summary>

<div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: 1fr 1fr; gap: 8px; margin: 1em 0;">
  <img src="/images/baguette/2-let-rise-with-lid.jpg" alt="Before rising" style="width: 100%; height: 250px; object-fit: cover; border-radius: 4px;">
  <img src="/images/baguette/3-morning-result-of-rise.jpg" alt="After rising" style="width: 100%; height: 250px; object-fit: cover; border-radius: 4px;">
</div>

</details>

**3.** Preheat the oven to 230°C and line rack with baking paper.

**4.** Dust flour over the worktop and carefully turn the dough out onto it.

<details>
<summary>show photos</summary>

![Flour the work surface](/images/baguette/4-flour-work-surface.jpg)

![Dough on work surface](/images/baguette/6-dough-on-work-surface.jpg)

</details>

**5.** Dust more flour over the top of the dough.

<details>
<summary>show photo</summary>

![Flour on top of dough](/images/baguette/7-flour-on-dough.jpg)

</details>

**6.** Divide into 4 equal pieces. Gently shape each piece into a baguette. Avoid pressing out the air.

<details>
<summary>show photo</summary>

![Shape into baguettes](/images/baguette/8-make-into-baguette-shapes.jpg)

</details>

**7.** Flip and stretch out as long as the rack.

<details>
<summary>show photo</summary>

![Ready for the oven](/images/baguette/9-put-onto-rack.jpg)

</details>

**8.** Bake for 25 to 30 minutes until golden.
